From CP's Desk

It is my proud privilege to lead Police Commissionerate in Twin Cities of Bhubaneswar and Cuttack. Commissionerate encompasses prestigious Capital City of Bhubaneswar and millennium City of Cuttack. Growing urbanization, establishment of eminent educational institutions, spread of service industries have led to vast expansion of the twin City. Read More >>
